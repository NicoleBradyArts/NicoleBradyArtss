I Love You, You Love Me:

I Love You, You Love Me is an exploration of loneliness following the day in the life of a lonely clown. It is a dance theatre experience that blurs the lines between drab reality and fantastical daydreams. This beautiful show touches on loneliness, grief, and self-expression with only two lines of dialogue and lots and lots of clownery; the most genuine expression of learning self-love. This original work was Brady’s directorial debut in February of 2023, produced by Bridges Theatre Company at Point Park.

Days Without End:

Days Without End by Eugene O'Neill was produced by Pinnacle Productions at Point Park in November of 2024 as Brady’s senior capstone project. She took the 1930’s drama and transformed it into an uncanny valley horror experience.
