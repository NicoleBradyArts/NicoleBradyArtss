What She's Up To

Nicole Brady has been keeping busy with an exciting variety of creative projects spanning theater, voice work, and more. Recently, she performed as part of the cast for In The Woods, Still I Hear Them at the Spring 2024 RLM One Acts Festival, produced by Bridges Theatre Company, where she collaborated with a talented team of directors, designers, and actors.

Her voice acting talents shine in The Proposal by Anton Chekov, directed by Amy Rose Scoggins, where she lent her voice to the character of Stepan Stepanovitch Chubukov alongside a talented voice cast.

She also took the stage for The Seasons with Evening Crane Theatre at the 2024 Pittsburgh Fringe Festival, sharing the spotlight with a diverse ensemble and contributing to a production crafted by M. Seebold and a skilled team of designers.
